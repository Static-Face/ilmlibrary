Qawaid Al Arba: The Four Fundamental Principles

Author: Shaykh Muhammad bin Abdul-Wahhab, Dr Salih al-Fawzan

Description: The is the treatise “The Four Principles” by the Imam, al-Mujaddid (reformer), Shaykh Muhammad Ibn Abdul Wahhab which deals with the matter of shirk and the creed of its people. It is also accompanied by a commentary by the noble Shaykh Salih Al-Fawzan.
