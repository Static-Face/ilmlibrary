The Aqeedah of Imam al Bukhari

Author: Abu Iyaad Amjad ibn Muhammad Rafiq

Translator: Abu Iyaadh Amjad Rafiq, Abu Talha Dawud Burbank

Description: This book is a translation of some of the points of the Creed of Ahlus-Sunnah Wal Jamaa’ah. The text is as relevant today as it was over one thousand years ago, if not more so, since confussion regarding matters of Manhaj and Aqeedah (Methodology and Creed) has increased. So know that success lies in sticking to the way of the Salaf as Imaam Maalik Bin Anas (Died 179 AH) said, “The latter part of this Ummah will not be rectified except by that which rectified its earliest part”. So compare the Imaam Al-Bukhaaree contained in this treatise to the speech of the caller to Islaam today, and you will see who in reality is in accordance to Islaam as it was understood by the earliest generations.
