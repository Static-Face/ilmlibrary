Sharh Kashf Ash-Shubuhaat - Explonation of Removal of Doubts

Author: Shaykh Muhammad ibn Abdul-Wahhab, Shaykh Muhammad Ibn Salih Al-Uthaimin

Translator: Qasim Mutiva

Description: This is a concise explanation of the book of Shaykhul-Islam Muhammad Ibn Abdul Wahhab entitled, Removing the Doubts of the People of Shirk. In this book, the author mentions ten-odd doubts from the people of Shirk and answers them with excellent answers, relying upon textual proof along with an easily comprehensible meaning and clear expression. I ask Allah (subhaanahu wa taâ€™aalaa) to reward him for that and to benefit the servants through it. Indeed, Allah is capable of all things.
