Tawassul: Seeking A Means Of Nearness To Allah

Author: Shaykh Muhammad Naasiruddeen al-Albaanee

Translator: Aboo Talhah Dawood ibn Ronlad Burbank

Description: The basis for this book are two lectures which were delivered by Imam Muhammad Nasirud-Din Al-Albani in which he dealt with the topic of al-tawassul and all its various aspects. The people are in great confusion about the matter of Tawassul and its rulings in the religion, and they greatly differ concerning it, some declaring it lawful and other prohibiting it, some going to extremes and others being over-lenient. This book talks about what Tawassul is, what are its types, what is the meaning of the Aayaat and the ahaadeeth which mention it, and what is the correct ruling for it in Islaam.
