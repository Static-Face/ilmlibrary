Warning Against the Fitnah of Takfeer

Author: Shaykh Muhammad Nasir ud-Deen al-Albaani

Translator: Abbas Abu Yahya

Description: "Warning Against the Fitnah of Takfeer" by Muhammad Nasir ud-Deen al-Albaani addresses the dangers and misconceptions surrounding the practice of Takfeer, which involves declaring a Muslim to be a non-believer (kafir). The book emphasizes that Takfeer is a serious issue that has historical roots in the Khawarij sect and has re-emerged among certain modern groups. Al-Albaani highlights the importance of deep knowledge and proper understanding of Sharia to avoid falling into this error. He stresses that declaring someone a kafir should not be based on superficial judgments or individual interpretations but must align with the teachings of the Quran, Sunnah, and the understanding of the Salaf (righteous predecessors). The book calls for adherence to the path of the Companions of the Prophet and warns against the consequences of misapplying Takfeer, which can lead to discord and strife within the Muslim community.
