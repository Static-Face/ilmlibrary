The Foundations of Islam

Author: Imam Muhammad Ibn Abdul Wahab

Translator: Abu Rumaysah

Description: The Foundation of Islam By Imam Muhammed ibn Abdul Wahhab is an important collection dealing with essential components of Muslim belief and identity. The first is the outstanding work, Masa il Al Jahiliyyah in which the author list 128 characteristics, current amongst the people of the time of ignorance, which were opposed by the messenger of Allah.
