Sharh As-Sunnah: The Explanation of the Sunnah Vol. 2

Author: Imam al-Barbahaaree, Dr. Salih al-Fawzan

Translator: Abdus-Sami Abdus-Salam

Description: Sharh as-Sunnah, Explanation of The Sunnah By Imam al-Barbahari A Gift to the Reader in Annotation of Sharh as-Sunnah : The Explanation of The Sunnah This is the highly beneficial two volume work in explanation of the famous works by Imam al-Barbahari (d.329h) by the esteemed & noble Shaykh, Dr Saalih al- Fawzan. Its importance can be seen by virtue of its being compiled in the early times; as it is one of the books of the earliest Salaf - those who lived in the same period with the great Imams, acquired knowledge from them and reported their pure Aqidah (creed).
