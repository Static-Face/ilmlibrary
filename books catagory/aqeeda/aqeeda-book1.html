Tawheed: Its Meaning & Categories, Ibn Uthaymeen

Author: Muhammad Ibn Saalih Al-‘Uthaymeen

Translator: Abu az-Zubayr Harrison

Description: "Tawheed: Its Meaning & Categories" by Muhammad Ibn Saalih Al-‘Uthaymeen provides an in-depth explanation of the concept of Tawheed in Islam, which means the oneness of Allah. The book categorizes Tawheed into three distinct parts: Tawheed Ar-Ruboobiyyah (the oneness of Allah’s lordship), Tawheed Al-Uloohiyyah (the oneness of Allah’s right to be worshipped), and Tawheed Al-Asmaa was-Sifaat (the oneness of Allah’s names and attributes). Each category is meticulously detailed with Quranic verses and Hadiths to illustrate the principles of affirming Allah’s uniqueness in creation, worship, and divine attributes, while negating any form of association with Him.
