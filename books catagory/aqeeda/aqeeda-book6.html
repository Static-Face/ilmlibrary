Usool As Sittah: Six Fundamental Principles

Author: Shaykh Muhammad bin Abdul-Wahhab, Dr Salih al-Fawzan

Translator: ‘Abdullah Hylton

Description: This is a translation of Shaykh Ibn Abdul-Wahhaabs book: Usul As-Sitta (The Six Fundamental Principles) in which he identifies the six greatest factors that distinguish the Saved Sect from its counterparts. Despite being over 200 years old, the reader will – with little observation, realise that these principles are still very much applicable in our time, and it is as though Ibn Abd al-Wahhaab is addressing the Islamic community of today. This is the second edition of this publication which has undergone much revision. For the benefit of the student of knowledge and the learner of the Arabic language, a parallel translation has been introduced to help with following and studying the text. The translation has been kept as simple as possible in order to cater to a wider audience. The speech of Muhammad ibn ‘Abd al-Wahhab is presented adjacent to: ‘The Text’, whilst the remaining is that of Shaykh Saalih Al-Fawzan
