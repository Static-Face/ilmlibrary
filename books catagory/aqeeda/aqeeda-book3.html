The Creed of The Four Imams

Author: Dr Muhammad Ibn Abd al-Rahman al-Khumayyis

Translator: Maaz Qureshi

Description: Enlightening clarification from the four Imams themselves – from their own words – about the correct position on vital issues of creed, such as tawheed, eemaan and qadar as well as the position of Ahlus-Sunnah on theological rhetoric and the honour and respect due to the Companions of the Prophet (Peace and blessings of Allah be upon him). It is sincerely hoped that all Muslims who claim adherence to the teachings of these great Imams will truly embrace the most important aspect of their teachings the correct beliefs they understood from the texts of the Book and the Sunnah based on the understanding of the Companions and their followers.
