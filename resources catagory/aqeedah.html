Beginner's Guide to Aqeedah

Step-by-Step Guide for New Muslims to Learn Aqeedah

This guide is designed to help new Muslims understand the basics of Aqeedah. Follow the steps below to build a strong foundation in your faith.

Beginner Books

Start with these foundational books to get an introduction to Aqeedah:

  • Tawheed: Its Meaning & Categories - An introduction to Tawheed and Aqeedah by Shaykh Al-â€˜Uthaymeen
  • Usool Al Thalatha - An essential text outlining the three fundamental principles every Muslim should know.
  • Qawaid Al Arba'a - A concise work discussing four important principles concerning the understanding of monotheism.
  • Nawawid Al Islam - A book addressing nullifiers of Islam and clarifying what can invalidate one's faith.

Intermediate Books

Once you have a basic understanding, move on to these intermediate texts:

  • Kitab at-Tauhid - A comprehensive text that explores the concept of Tawheed, the oneness of Allah, in depth.
  • Sharh Kashf al Shubahat - A book that addresses common misconceptions about Islamic monotheism and provides clear explanations.
  • Usool Al Sittah - This book covers six fundamental principles necessary for understanding Islam properly.

Advanced Books

For a deeper understanding, study these advanced works:

  • Aqidat al Wasitiyah Vol. 1 - A detailed treatise on Islamic creed, covering various aspects of belief in a systematic manner.
  • Aqidat al Wasitiyah Vol. 2 - Second volume of Aqidat al Wasitiyah
  • Masa'il al Jahiliyah - This book discusses the characteristics of pre-Islamic ignorance and contrasts them with Islamic teachings.
  • Sharh as Sunnah Vol. 1 - An explanation of the Sunnah, covering the traditional and orthodox beliefs of Islam.
  • Sharh as Sunnah Vol. 2 - Second volume of The Explanation of the Sunnah
